The goal of this project

I'm trying to find an explanation, why the most popular songs/artists sound the same.

Similarities between artists/songs

The most streamed songs BPM is around 120. That checks out with the high "Danceability" measures, because thats not too slow, neither too fast. Ideal for clubs, events. Thats in a correlation with the high "Energy", and "Valence" values.

The TOP Artists rarely make pure instrumental songs.
They are all singers, and people can connect to a human voice more easily.

Songs in a major key get more streams on average, and the performers use this. From the analysed songs 475 were in Major, and 383 in Minor.
Thats a 25% difference in quantity.
